Privacy Policy

Explorer App  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the Explorer App collects, why we collect it, how it is stored, and the controls you have over it. Explorer is an Android application that tracks your routes and lets you enrich them with photos and audio notes captured along the way.

📍 Information We Collect

Explorer collects the following categories of data when you use the app:

  • Precise location data — recorded continuously to trace your route as you move, including while the app is running in the background.
  • Photos — images captured through the in-app camera, pinned to the point on your route where they were taken.
  • Audio recordings — voice notes dictated during a route, associated with your position at the time of recording.
  • Route metadata — details such as start time, duration, and distance, stored alongside your recorded path.
Background location: Explorer requests the Background Location permission on Android. This allows the app to continue recording your route accurately even when you switch to another app or lock your screen — essential for tracking a complete journey.

🔍 How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected solely to provide and improve the core features of Explorer:

  • Recording and displaying your routes on a map.
  • Attaching photos and audio notes to specific points along a route.
  • Showing route statistics such as distance, duration, and path.
  • Allowing you to browse and replay past routes.
  • Storing your routes and media so they are available the next time you open the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the core functionality described above.

💾 Data Storage Stays on Your Device

All personal data — including your recorded routes, location history, photos, and audio notes — is stored exclusively on your device. None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party.

The only information that leaves your device is anonymous, aggregate usage statistics — for example, the total number of routes created. These figures contain no location points, no media, and nothing that could identify you or your activity.

If you use Android's automatic backup, your app data may be backed up to your Google account according to the permissions you have granted at the OS level. We recommend reviewing your Android backup settings if you have concerns about this.

🔐 Permissions Explained

Explorer requests the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION — to record your precise GPS position, including when the app is in the background.
  • CAMERA — to take photos that are pinned to points along your route.
  • RECORD_AUDIO — to capture voice notes associated with points along your route.
  • READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (where applicable) — to save photos and audio files to your device.

You can revoke any permission at any time through your device's Settings → Apps → Explorer → Permissions. Revoking a permission will disable the associated feature but will not affect data already saved.

🗑️ Data Retention & Deletion

Your data lives entirely on your device and is retained for as long as the app is installed. You can delete individual routes, photos, or audio notes at any time from within Explorer.

To erase all app data at once, go to Settings → Apps → Explorer → Storage → Clear Data on your Android device, or simply uninstall the app. Because no personal data is stored on our servers, there is nothing additional for us to delete on your behalf.

👶 Children's Privacy

Explorer is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

⚖️ Your Rights

Because all personal data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can view, edit, or delete your data directly within the app, or wipe everything via Settings → Apps → Explorer → Storage → Clear Data.

The only data we hold is anonymous aggregate statistics (such as total route counts) which cannot be linked back to you. If you have any questions about how this data is used, feel free to contact us using the details below.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process anonymous analytics on the lawful basis of legitimate interests in understanding how the app is used at an aggregate level.

📋 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Explorer after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
